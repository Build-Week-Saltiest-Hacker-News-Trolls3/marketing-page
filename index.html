What it does:

Have you ever been scrolled down the Hacker News website and realized that there are a lot of salty people? Have you ever wanted to only read those salty comments for your own amusement? Hacker News Saltiest Trolls is the revolutionary app that strives to rank the most active users by level of saltiness for your own personal entertainment and for your convenience.
